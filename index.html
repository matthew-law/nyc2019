New York City 2019 Fieldwork Report


This website looks at places in which narratives of immigration are recounted.
It is based on my experiences of visiting six sites in New York in November 2019.

It was produced as coursework for the North American Urban Geographies module in my Geography degree at the University of Nottingham, and is accompanied by a supporting statement, which attempts to justify my decision to make a website instead of writing an essay.
